## 1. Company

- **Name:** Vital Synergy
- **Sector:** Mechanical and electrical engineering (M&E)
- **Positioning:** Trusted partner for M&E projects; innovation, sustainability, high-quality service.

*Source: [Brand Tone and Voice Guide](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BD7232C3A-3FB5-4DDC-BCB7-90AB496D5BFF%7D&file=Vital%20Synergy%20%E2%80%93%20Brand%20Tone%20and%20Voice%20Guide.docx)*

## 2. Brand personality (five pillars)

- **Professional** — clear, concise, informed; no unnecessary jargon.
- **Approachable** — friendly and welcoming while retaining authority.
- **Reliable** — dependable, consistent, solution focused.
- **Forward-thinking** — innovative, current on industry advancements.
- **Community minded** — supportive of local and regional initiatives.

*Source: [Brand Tone and Voice Guide](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BD7232C3A-3FB5-4DDC-BCB7-90AB496D5BFF%7D&file=Vital%20Synergy%20%E2%80%93%20Brand%20Tone%20and%20Voice%20Guide.docx)*

## 3. Voice principles

- Clarity over cleverness — plain English; avoid jargon and acronyms unless defined.
- Confidence without arrogance — lead with expertise, stay humble.
- Warmth with professionalism — friendly tone, never casual.
- Consistency across channels — same voice in proposals, emails, social, web.

*Source: [Brand Tone and Voice Guide](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BD7232C3A-3FB5-4DDC-BCB7-90AB496D5BFF%7D&file=Vital%20Synergy%20%E2%80%93%20Brand%20Tone%20and%20Voice%20Guide.docx)*

## 4. Tone by scenario

| Scenario | Tone |
|----------|------|
| Client proposals / tenders | Confident, precise, evidence-led |
| Marketing collateral | Warm, benefits-led, plain English |
| Social media | Conversational but never flippant; community minded |
| Internal comms | Clear, supportive, action-oriented |
| Crisis / sensitive | Calm, factual, empathetic |

*Source: [Brand Tone and Voice Guide](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BD7232C3A-3FB5-4DDC-BCB7-90AB496D5BFF%7D&file=Vital%20Synergy%20%E2%80%93%20Brand%20Tone%20and%20Voice%20Guide.docx)*

## 5. Language rules

- **Spelling:** UK English (organise, colour, programme, specialise).
- **Punctuation:** NO em dashes. Use commas, semicolons, or full stops.
- **Preferred terms:** "bespoke" (not "tailored"), "engaging" (not "exciting"), "expertise" (not "experience" when meaning skill).
- **Voice:** active. ("Our team delivered…" not "It was delivered by our team…")
- **Acronyms:** spell out on first use, then abbreviate. M&E (Mechanical and Electrical).
- **Numbers:** spell out one to nine; use figures for 10+.

*Source: [Brand Tone and Voice Guide](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BD7232C3A-3FB5-4DDC-BCB7-90AB496D5BFF%7D&file=Vital%20Synergy%20%E2%80%93%20Brand%20Tone%20and%20Voice%20Guide.docx)*

## 6. Image & consent rules

- Written consent required for **any** identifiable individual in marketing imagery.
- Parental/guardian consent required for anyone under 18.
- Consent forms must be stored centrally and reviewed when re-using imagery.
- Withdraw imagery on request without delay.
- Never use unclear, low quality, or out-of-context images of people.
- Site/safety: subjects must wear correct PPE; no unsafe practices visible.

*Source: [Image and Consent Guidelines](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BC2A1274F-A8F8-45A9-8983-79A61BBB200F%7D&file=Image%20and%20Consent%20Guidelines.docx)*

## 7. Prohibited practices (images)

- No use of imagery without documented consent.
- No stock images that misrepresent our actual work or team.
- No imagery showing unsafe practices, missing PPE, or hazards.
- No imagery that could embarrass, mislead, or harm an individual.
- No re-use of consented imagery beyond the agreed scope.

*Source: [Image and Consent Guidelines](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BC2A1274F-A8F8-45A9-8983-79A61BBB200F%7D&file=Image%20and%20Consent%20Guidelines.docx)*

## 8. Social media — platforms & approval

- **Active platforms:** LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube.
- All posts require Marketing approval before publishing.
- Sensitive topics (incidents, client matters, politics) require Director sign-off.
- Maintain a content calendar; align with the Marketing Strategy.

*Source: [Social Media SOPs](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BDABC5069-0986-47AA-9398-583D12FE3542%7D&file=Social%20Media%20SOPs.docx)*

## 9. Social media — engagement do's & don'ts

**Do**
- Respond to comments and DMs within one business day.
- Stay on-brand: professional, approachable, community minded.
- Escalate complaints, safety, or compliance issues to Marketing lead.
- Use approved hashtags only.

**Don't**
- Engage in political, religious, or contentious debate.
- Reply when angry, defensive, or unsure — escalate instead.
- Share client information without written approval.
- Post outside the approval workflow.

*Source: [Social Media SOPs](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BDABC5069-0986-47AA-9398-583D12FE3542%7D&file=Social%20Media%20SOPs.docx)*

## 10. Security & account hygiene

- All social accounts require 2FA (two-factor authentication).
- Access limited to approved Marketing personnel.
- Passwords stored in approved password manager.
- Off-boarding: revoke access immediately when a team member leaves.

*Source: [Social Media SOPs](https://vitalheating.sharepoint.com/sites/Marketing/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7BDABC5069-0986-47AA-9398-583D12FE3542%7D&file=Social%20Media%20SOPs.docx)*
